Basic Training

Basic training is your introduction to EMDR therapy and a requirement to become certified in this evidence based therapy for post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD). There are many organizations and solo trainers who provide this training. Every training must include 20 hours of lecture, 20 hours of practicum and 10 hours of consultation.
